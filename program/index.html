Workshop

The conference will host workshops on the afternoon of August 31st (Saturday), focusing on topics related to smart cities and geospatial big data. Experts and scholars will share their thoughts, experiences, and practical operations on smart community construction, geospatial data analysis, and large language models applied to urban research. We hope these workshops will provide valuable insights and inspiration for researchers and professionals working in related fields.
